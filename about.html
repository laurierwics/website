About Us

Laurier Women in Computer Science (LWiCS) is a student-run organization at Wilfrid Laurier University that focuses on empowering women studying both computer science and mathematics. We strive to build a supportive community and provide resources through our events and initiatives. Our club is open to anybody looking to promote equality and diversity in tech.

How To Join

To be considered a general member of the club, you MUST attend two events per semester.
