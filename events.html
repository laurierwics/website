Career & Mentorship Workshops

Community Building Events

These career panels give an opportunity for students to interact with professionals from a variety of tech companies and learn about what it takes to prepare and be successful in the field. Some of the panels we've had in the past are our Tech Recruiter Panel, UX Professional Panel, Women in Data Science Panel, and many more.

These workshops are aimed at providing tips on writing a resume, pitching yourself in an interview, and personal branding. We have held some events in the past where tech recruiters worked with students individually to review their resumes and answer any career related questions they had. We have also held events where tech professionals answer student's career questions and provide tips and advice about working in the tech industry.

These events are aimed at fostering a safe, inclusive and diverse space. These events create the opportunity for computer science students to meet new people, promote gender equity, and build community. Past events have included beginning of term socials, game nights, and study sessions.
